Empowering Learning Through Interactive Quizzes

Quiz-O-Buzz is the ultimate platform for teachers to evaluate students and for students to challenge themselves, seamlessly and efficiently.

The Problem

Traditional classroom-based quiz methods often involve manual preparation, paper-based distribution, and manual evaluation of student responses. These processes can be time-consuming, inefficient, and difficult to manage, particularly when maintaining accurate records of student performance. Furthermore, traditional methods lack real-time feedback mechanisms and convenient accessibility for both teachers and students.

The Solution

We recognized the need for a simple and efficient digital solution that enables teachers to create quizzes effortlessly while allowing students to participate in assessments online. The objective of Quiz-O-Buzz is to be that web-based quiz platform—facilitating quiz creation, participation, and automated evaluation to radically improve efficiency, accessibility, and performance tracking through modern web technologies.

👨‍🏫 For Teachers

  • Intuitive Quiz Builder with easy editing
  • Customizable Time Limits down to the minute
  • Secure, easily Shareable Links for quick distribution
  • Seamless dashboard to track past score records

👨‍🎓 For Students

  • Frictionless interface to attempt quizzes
  • Real-time visual Countdown Timer
  • Instant score calculation and feedback
  • Personalized dashboard to view progress

How It Works

1

Create

Teachers craft customized quizzes with ease, adding questions, options, and defining exact time limits.

2

Share

Upon saving, a unique Quiz Link is generated. Teachers share this link directly with their class.

3

Analyze

Students attempt the quiz and scores are calculated instantly and safely stored on the website.
